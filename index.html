About Me

I am an extremely motivated individual who is constantly looking to develop my skills and grow professionally. I am confident in my ability to come up with interesting ideas to make the impossible possible. I am goal-driven, self-disciplined, and a fast learner that thrives under pressure. I have excellent communication skills which are executed in a professional and friendly manner which were further developed through the completion of my undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in psychology.

My passion for technology started at a young age when I was given my first console - the PlayStation 1. This was a catalyst for merging myself into the tech industry because I always wanted to keep up to date with the latest cellphone and gaming consoles that were being released. I also used to do IT in primary school and this further intensified my passion for the tech industry given how quickly it grasped my attention and played on my need and want to learn about anything and everything technology-based.

Some of my hobbies and activities include Sports - particularly Football and Formula 1, Mobile/Console/PC Gaming, IT, Community Service where I educate individuals surrounding Mental Health and the stigma associated, Psychology, and Cooking. A lot of these were developed at a young age but my hobbies within Psychology were mainly defined during my studies where I saw myself growing into a Mental Health Activist. I found myself wanting to destignmatise the negative connatations surrounding Mental Health.
